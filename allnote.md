HOW TO PREPARE？

1. Read this for gist (without a dictionary). Think about andunderline / highlight key ideas and arguments.
阅读本文的要点(不需要字典)。思考并强调关键的观点和论点

2. Read again for further detail - you can check unknown words in a dictionary and make notes on the text in any way that you wish.

##### thesis statement 论文陈述
用简洁明了的句子，用于概括论文的主要观点和论证方向

- one or two sentences, towards the end of the introduction 一到两句话，在引言的末尾
- an idea with an opinion / judgement that can be discussed, argued and explained with supporting evidence 一个带有观点/判断的想法，可以用支持性的证据进行讨论、论证和解释
- important because it signposts the argument of the author to the reader and guides the argument within the essay. 重要的是隐私它向读者指明了作者的论点，并指导了文章中的论点。

A thesis statement is NOT:
- a topic (eg. alternative education)
- a fact 
- a statement expressing 2 sides of an argument equally 平等地表达了一个论点的两方面的陈述
